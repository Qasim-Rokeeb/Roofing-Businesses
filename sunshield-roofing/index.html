Deflecting the Intense Lagos Sun

Standard black zinc sheets absorb over 85% of solar heat radiation, converting your roof space into a high-temperature radiator that forces your cooling units to run continuously.

Our proprietary **SunShield technology** utilizes ceramic-formulated reflective finishes and multi-layer thermal foil barriers. By reflecting 90% of solar heat rays, we keep your attic cool, reducing thermal stress on your structure and lowering energy draw immediately.
