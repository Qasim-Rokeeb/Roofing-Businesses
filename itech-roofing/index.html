Our Services

Roofing Solutions Tailored for Nigeria

Comprehensive engineering services, from initial timber construction to premium finish and leak repairs.

Roof Installation

Complete structural roofing installation for residential villas, commercial structures, and industrial warehouses. We handle structural timber carpentry, truss calculation, spacing alignments, and secure sheeting installation.

  • Hardwood chemical-treated timber trusses
  • High-wind resistance anchoring techniques
  • Engineered alignment inspections

Roof Repair & Restoration

Professional diagnostic inspection and repair for sagging roof profiles, broken trusses, and persistent water leaks. We apply specialized waterproof liquid polyurethane membranes on complex roof valleys and parapet joints.

  • Precise leakage tracing and patching
  • Damaged timber truss reinforcing/swapping
  • Silicone waterproofing coatings

Roofing Sheet Supplies

Supplier of premium Aluzinc roofing sheets in multiple designs. We offer Longspan, Step-tiles, Metrocopo, and Camargo profiles, tailored directly to your requested length. High thickness specifications (0.45mm - 0.55mm) ensure durability.

  • Genuine Aluzinc high-grade steel core
  • Multiple color coatings (matte/glossy)
  • Cut-to-length customization to minimize waste

Gerald (Stone Coated Tiles)

Distributor and installer of premium Gerald stone-coated metal roof tiles. These tiles combine the strength of structural steel with beautiful textured volcanic stone chips. Excellent for noise reduction, aesthetic elegance, and thermal insulation.

  • Bond, Classic, Shingle, and Shake profiles
  • Non-fading UV resistant natural stone granules
  • Overlocking interlocks to resist storm uplift
