DIAGNOSIS: ROOF RIDGE LINE FAULT

Leaking from the ridge is typically caused by rusted cap nails, wind-blown ridge caps, or dried mortar joint cracking. During storms, rain gets forced underneath the ridge cap.
REQUIRED REMEDY:
Remove old cap plates, apply custom high-build elastomeric sealant, and lock in new heavy-gauge ridge caps.
