FAQ's

Common Roofing Questions

Answering structural specifications, distribution channels, and installation metrics.

What profiles of stone coated tiles do you supply?

▼
We supply genuine Bond, Classic, Shingle, Shake, and Milano profiles in wholesales and retails. All tiles are treated with natural UV‑resistant stone granules to guarantee a non‑fading surface under intense sun.

Do you deliver materials nationwide across Nigeria?

▼
Yes, Authenticrooftiles Intercontinental Limited operates 35 branches and coordinates heavy delivery trucks to transport wholesale orders to any construction site nationwide in Nigeria.

What is the standard structural warranty on your installations?

▼
We provide a 25+ year warranty on timber framing structures and stone‑coated tile installs, guaranteeing protection against leakage and storm damage.

What accessories do you supply for a complete roof installation?

▼
We supply Camargo aluminum sheets, color‑coated drive nails, chemical wood treatments, transparent sheet inserts, and rain‑water collector systems.
